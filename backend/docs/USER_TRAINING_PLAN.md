# ScrollUniversity AI Services User Training Plan

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the comprehensive training plan for rolling out ScrollUniversity's AI automation services to all user groups: faculty, students, support staff, and administrators.

**Training Timeline**: 4 weeks
**Target Audience**: 1,000+ users
**Delivery Methods**: Online, in-person, self-paced
**Success Metrics**: 90% completion rate, 85% satisfaction score

---

## Training Objectives

### Overall Goals
1. Enable all users to effectively use AI services
2. Ensure understanding of AI capabilities and limitations
3. Maintain academic integrity and theological alignment
4. Achieve 90% user adoption within 3 months
5. Reduce support tickets by 50%

### User-Specific Goals

**Faculty**:
- Use AI teaching assistant effectively
- Grade assignments with AI assistance
- Create content using AI tools
- Monitor AI quality and provide feedback

**Students**:
- Access 24/7 AI support
- Use AI tutor ethically
- Leverage research assistant
- Understand academic integrity policies

**Support Staff**:
- Monitor AI chatbot performance
- Handle escalations efficiently
- Improve knowledge base
- Provide excellent customer service

**Administrators**:
- Monitor system health and costs
- Ensure quality and compliance
- Generate reports and insights
- Make data-driven decisions

## Training Delivery Methods

### 1. Live Training

**In-Person Workshops**
- **Pros**: Hands-on practice, immediate feedback, networking
- **Cons**: Scheduling challenges, limited capacity
- **Best For**: Administrators, support staff, faculty

**Live Webinars**
- **Pros**: Scalable, convenient, recorded for later
- **Cons**: Less interactive, technical issues
- **Best For**: Large group orientations, student training

### 2. Self-Paced Learning

**Online Learning Platform**
- **Features**:
  - Video tutorials
  - Interactive quizzes
  - Progress tracking
  - Certificates of completion
- **Best For**: Students, busy faculty, ongoing training

**Documentation Portal**
- **Features**:
  - Searchable knowledge base
  - Step-by-step guides
  - FAQ section
  - Community forum
- **Best For**: Reference, troubleshooting, advanced users

### 3. Hands-On Practice

**Sandbox Environment**
- **Features**:
  - Safe testing environment
  - Sample data
  - No cost or consequences
  - Guided exercises
- **Best For**: All users learning new features

**Peer Learning**
- **Features**:
  - Study groups
  - Peer mentoring
  - Best practices sharing
  - Community support
- **Best For**: Ongoing learning, advanced techniques

## Risk Management

### Potential Risks

**Low Attendance**
- **Mitigation**: Multiple session times, mandatory training, incentives
- **Contingency**: Self-paced alternatives, one-on-one sessions

**Technical Issues**
- **Mitigation**: Test all technology, backup plans, technical support
- **Contingency**: Reschedule, provide recordings, extend timeline

**User Resistance**
- **Mitigation**: Clear communication, demonstrate value, address concerns
- **Contingency**: Phased rollout, pilot programs, feedback loops

**Information Overload**
- **Mitigation**: Bite-sized modules, spaced learning, practice time
- **Contingency**: Extended timeline, additional support, simplified materials

**Quality Issues**
- **Mitigation**: Pilot testing, feedback collection, iterative improvement
- **Contingency**: Rapid updates, additional training, expert support
