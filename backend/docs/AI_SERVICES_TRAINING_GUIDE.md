## For Faculty

### Overview of Faculty AI Tools

Faculty have access to 7 AI-powered tools to enhance teaching:

1. **AI Teaching Assistant** - Answer student questions 24/7
2. **Automated Grading** - Grade assignments with detailed feedback
3. **Content Creation** - Generate lectures, assessments, and resources
4. **Discussion Grading** - Evaluate forum participation
5. **Quiz Generation** - Create assessments automatically
6. **Extension Management** - Process extension requests
7. **Faculty Dashboard** - Monitor AI usage and quality

### Getting Started

#### 1. Access Your Faculty Dashboard

1. Log in to ScrollUniversity
2. Navigate to **Faculty Portal** > **AI Tools**
3. Review your AI usage summary

#### 2. Set Up AI Teaching Assistant

**Purpose**: Answer common student questions automatically

**Setup Steps**:
1. Go to **AI Tools** > **Teaching Assistant**
2. Upload your course materials (syllabus, lecture notes, FAQs)
3. Set confidence threshold (recommended: 80%)
4. Enable/disable for specific courses
5. Review and approve AI responses before they're sent

**Best Practices**:
- Upload comprehensive course materials
- Review AI responses weekly
- Adjust confidence threshold based on accuracy
- Provide feedback on incorrect responses

**Example Use Cases**:
- "When is the assignment due?"
- "What topics are covered in Module 3?"
- "How do I submit my project?"
- "What's the grading rubric for essays?"

#### 3. Use Automated Grading

**Purpose**: Grade assignments faster with consistent, detailed feedback

**How It Works**:
1. Student submits assignment
2. AI grades based on your rubric
3. You review AI grade and feedback
4. Approve, modify, or override

**Supported Assignment Types**:
- **Code**: Correctness, efficiency, style, documentation
- **Essays**: Thesis, argument, evidence, writing quality
- **Math**: Methodology, correctness, conceptual understanding
- **Projects**: Completeness, creativity, technical execution

**Grading Workflow**:

```
Student Submission
       ↓
AI Analysis (2-5 minutes)
       ↓
AI Grade + Detailed Feedback
       ↓
Faculty Review Queue (if confidence < 85%)
       ↓
Faculty Approval/Modification
       ↓
Grade Released to Student
```

**Best Practices**:
- Create detailed rubrics for better AI grading
- Review low-confidence grades carefully
- Provide feedback on AI grading accuracy
- Use AI for initial grading, human for final review

**Confidence Levels**:
- **90-100%**: High confidence, minimal review needed
- **85-89%**: Medium confidence, quick review recommended
- **<85%**: Low confidence, thorough review required

**Content Types**:

### Managing AI Services

#### Enable/Disable Services

```bash
# Via Admin Dashboard
Settings > AI Services > [Service Name] > Toggle

# Via API
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/api/admin/services/toggle \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $ADMIN_TOKEN" \
  -d '{"service": "GradingService", "enabled": false}'
```

#### Adjust Budgets

```bash
# Set monthly budget
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/api/admin/budget/set \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $ADMIN_TOKEN" \
  -d '{"monthly": 10000}'

# Set service-specific budget
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/api/admin/budget/service \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $ADMIN_TOKEN" \
  -d '{"service": "GradingService", "monthly": 2000}'
```

#### Configure Quality Thresholds

```bash
# Set minimum accuracy
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/api/admin/quality/thresholds \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $ADMIN_TOKEN" \
  -d '{"minAccuracy": 0.90, "minConfidence": 0.85}'
```

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is my data safe with AI?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted, FERPA/GDPR compliant, and never shared with third parties.

**Q: Can AI replace teachers?**
A: No. AI assists teachers, allowing them to focus on mentoring and complex instruction.

**Q: How accurate is the AI?**
A: AI accuracy varies by service but averages >90%. All critical decisions are human-reviewed.

**Q: What if AI makes a mistake?**
A: Report it immediately. Humans review all AI decisions, and mistakes can be corrected.

**Q: Can I opt out of AI services?**
A: Some services are optional, others are core to the platform. Contact your advisor.

### Faculty Questions

**Q: Will AI grading replace my job?**
A: No. AI handles initial grading; you make final decisions and provide mentorship.

**Q: How long does AI grading take?**
A: 2-5 minutes per submission, compared to 15-30 minutes manually.

**Q: Can I customize AI grading rubrics?**
A: Yes. Create detailed rubrics for better AI grading.

**Q: What if I disagree with AI grade?**
A: You can always override AI grades. Your decision is final.

### Student Questions

**Q: Will AI detect if I use ChatGPT?**
A: Yes. We use advanced AI detection tools. Don't submit AI-generated work.

**Q: Can I use AI tutor during exams?**
A: Only if explicitly permitted by your instructor.

**Q: Is AI tutoring considered cheating?**
A: No, when used to understand concepts. Yes, if you copy answers.

**Q: How do I know if AI answer is correct?**
A: AI provides sources. Verify important information independently.

### Support Staff Questions

**Q: How do I know when to escalate?**
A: AI flags urgent issues and low-confidence responses automatically.

**Q: Can I edit AI responses?**
A: Yes. Always customize AI suggestions before sending.

**Q: What if AI gives wrong information?**
A: Correct it immediately and update the knowledge base.

### Administrator Questions

**Q: How much does AI cost?**
A: Approximately $8,000/month for full system ($8/student/year).

**Q: Can we customize AI services?**
A: Yes. Contact AI team for customization options.

**Q: How do we ensure theological alignment?**
A: All AI outputs are reviewed by theological alignment checker.

**Q: What's our disaster recovery plan?**
A: See Operations Runbook for complete DR procedures.
