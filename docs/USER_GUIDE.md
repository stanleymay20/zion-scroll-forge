## Getting Started

### System Requirements

**Web Application:**
- Modern web browser (Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+)
- Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps for video streaming)
- JavaScript enabled

**Mobile Application:**
- iOS 13+ or Android 8+
- 100MB free storage space
- Internet connection (offline mode available for downloaded content)

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit the Platform**
   - Navigate to https://scrolluniversity.org
   - Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"

2. **Choose Registration Method**
   - Email and password
   - Google account
   - Microsoft account

3. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Enter your full name
   - Select your role (Student, Faculty)
   - Add a profile picture (optional)
   - Set your timezone

4. **Verify Your Email**
   - Check your inbox for verification email
   - Click the verification link
   - You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## Course Enrollment

### Browsing the Course Catalog

1. **Access Courses**
   - Click "Courses" in the main navigation
   - Browse by faculty or use search

2. **Filter Options**
   - Faculty (Theology, Business, Technology, etc.)
   - Difficulty level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
   - Duration
   - Language
   - Price range

3. **Course Preview**
   - Watch introduction video
   - Review syllabus and modules
   - Check instructor credentials
   - Read student reviews

### Enrolling in a Course

1. **Select Payment Method**
   - Credit/Debit card (via Stripe)
   - ScrollCoin (blockchain currency)
   - Scholarship application

2. **Complete Enrollment**
   - Review course details
   - Accept terms and conditions
   - Process payment
   - Receive confirmation email

3. **Access Course Materials**
   - Course appears in "My Courses"
   - Immediate access to all modules
   - Download mobile app for offline access

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Login Problems:**
- **Forgot Password:** Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- **Email Not Verified:** Check spam folder, request new verification email
- **Account Locked:** Contact support after multiple failed attempts

**Video Playback Issues:**
- Check internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps)
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Disable browser extensions
- Update browser to latest version

**Payment Issues:**
- Verify card details
- Check sufficient funds
- Try different payment method
- Contact your bank
- Reach out to support

**Mobile App Issues:**
- Update to latest version
- Clear app cache
- Reinstall app
- Check device storage
- Verify internet connection

### Getting Help

**Support Channels:**
- **Help Center:** help.scrolluniversity.org

**Response Times:**
