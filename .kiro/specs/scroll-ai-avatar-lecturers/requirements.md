# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The AI Avatar Lecturers feature transforms traditional online education by introducing lifelike AI-powered virtual instructors that can deliver engaging lectures, interact with students in real-time, and conduct live Q&A sessions. These AI avatars will serve as the primary teaching interface for Scroll University courses, combining advanced AI conversation capabilities with realistic visual representation to create an immersive and personalized learning experience that aligns with our Christian educational mission.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a student, I want to attend lectures delivered by AI avatar instructors, so that I can receive engaging and personalized instruction that feels like learning from a real professor.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student accesses a course lecture THEN the system SHALL display a realistic AI avatar instructor
2. WHEN the AI avatar delivers content THEN the system SHALL synchronize lip movements with speech
3. WHEN presenting course material THEN the AI avatar SHALL use natural gestures and expressions
4. WHEN delivering spiritual content THEN the AI avatar SHALL maintain appropriate reverence and biblical alignment
5. WHEN a lecture begins THEN the AI avatar SHALL greet students personally and set a welcoming atmosphere

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a student, I want to ask questions during live lectures, so that I can get immediate clarification and engage actively with the learning material.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student raises their hand or types a question THEN the system SHALL notify the AI avatar
2. WHEN the AI avatar receives a question THEN it SHALL acknowledge the student by name
3. WHEN answering questions THEN the AI avatar SHALL provide contextually relevant responses based on course content
4. WHEN multiple questions are asked THEN the system SHALL queue them appropriately
5. WHEN a question requires spiritual guidance THEN the AI avatar SHALL incorporate biblical wisdom and prayer

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a student, I want to have one-on-one conversations with AI avatar instructors, so that I can receive personalized tutoring and spiritual mentorship.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student requests private consultation THEN the system SHALL initiate a personal session with the AI avatar
2. WHEN in private mode THEN the AI avatar SHALL adapt its teaching style to the student's learning preferences
3. WHEN discussing academic struggles THEN the AI avatar SHALL provide encouragement and practical study strategies
4. WHEN addressing spiritual questions THEN the AI avatar SHALL offer biblical counsel and prayer support
5. WHEN sessions end THEN the system SHALL save conversation history for continuity

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a faculty administrator, I want to customize AI avatar personalities and expertise, so that each course has an appropriately specialized and spiritually aligned instructor.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN creating a new course THEN the system SHALL allow selection of avatar appearance and personality traits
2. WHEN configuring expertise THEN the system SHALL enable subject matter specialization settings
3. WHEN setting spiritual alignment THEN the system SHALL ensure biblical worldview integration
4. WHEN updating avatar knowledge THEN the system SHALL allow real-time content and curriculum updates
5. WHEN managing multiple avatars THEN the system SHALL maintain consistent spiritual and academic standards

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a student, I want AI avatars to remember my learning progress and preferences, so that each interaction builds upon previous conversations and adapts to my needs.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student returns to class THEN the AI avatar SHALL reference previous interactions and progress
2. WHEN learning difficulties are identified THEN the system SHALL adjust teaching methods accordingly
3. WHEN spiritual growth milestones occur THEN the AI avatar SHALL acknowledge and celebrate progress
4. WHEN study patterns emerge THEN the system SHALL optimize scheduling and content delivery
5. WHEN academic goals are set THEN the AI avatar SHALL provide ongoing accountability and encouragement

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a student, I want to participate in group discussions facilitated by AI avatars, so that I can engage with peers while receiving expert guidance and moderation.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN group sessions begin THEN the AI avatar SHALL facilitate introductions and set discussion guidelines
2. WHEN managing discussions THEN the system SHALL ensure balanced participation among all students
3. WHEN conflicts arise THEN the AI avatar SHALL mediate with wisdom and Christian love
4. WHEN theological questions emerge THEN the system SHALL provide biblically sound guidance
5. WHEN sessions conclude THEN the AI avatar SHALL summarize key insights and assign follow-up actions

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want to monitor AI avatar interactions for quality and spiritual alignment, so that all student interactions maintain high educational and spiritual standards.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN avatars interact with students THEN the system SHALL log all conversations for review
2. WHEN spiritual content is discussed THEN the system SHALL validate biblical accuracy
3. WHEN inappropriate content is detected THEN the system SHALL immediately intervene and correct
4. WHEN quality issues arise THEN the system SHALL alert administrators for manual review
5. WHEN performance metrics are needed THEN the system SHALL provide comprehensive analytics and reports

### Requirement 8

**User Story:** As a student with accessibility needs, I want AI avatars to accommodate my learning requirements, so that I can fully participate in all educational activities regardless of my abilities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN accessibility needs are identified THEN the AI avatar SHALL adapt communication methods accordingly
2. WHEN visual impairments exist THEN the system SHALL provide enhanced audio descriptions and verbal cues
3. WHEN hearing difficulties are present THEN the system SHALL offer visual captions and sign language interpretation
4. WHEN learning disabilities are detected THEN the AI avatar SHALL modify pace and presentation style
5. WHEN language barriers exist THEN the system SHALL provide multilingual support and cultural adaptation
