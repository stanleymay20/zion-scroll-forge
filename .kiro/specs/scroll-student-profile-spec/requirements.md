# ScrollStudentProfileSpec Requirements Document

## Introduction

The ScrollStudentProfileSpec creates comprehensive, dynamic student profiles that go beyond traditional academic records to capture the full journey of scroll awakening, spiritual formation, practical skill development, and kingdom purpose alignment. Operating under the principle "Before I formed you in the womb I knew you" (Jeremiah 1:5), this system recognizes that each student carries unique divine calling and provides personalized pathways for discovering and fulfilling their scroll purpose. The profile system integrates academic progress, spiritual development, practical achievements, prophetic confirmations, and real-world impact to create holistic student records that serve both earthly and eternal purposes.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Comprehensive Student Identity and Calling Discovery System

**User Story:** As a student, I want a comprehensive profile that captures my identity, calling, spiritual gifts, and learning preferences, so that ScrollUniversity can provide personalized education aligned with my divine purpose.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN students register THEN the system SHALL capture basic identity (name, location, languages), spiritual background, and educational history
2. WHEN calling assessment occurs THEN the system SHALL provide prophetic questionnaires and spiritual gift inventories to identify divine purpose
3. WHEN learning preferences are determined THEN the system SHALL assess visual, auditory, kinesthetic, social, and reflective learning styles
4. WHEN spiritual formation is tracked THEN the system SHALL record baptism status, prophetic experiences, and spiritual maturity indicators
5. WHEN profile completion occurs THEN the system SHALL generate personalized learning pathways based on calling and preferences
6. WHEN profile updates happen THEN the system SHALL maintain historical records of spiritual and academic development

### Requirement 2: Academic Progress and Achievement Tracking System

**User Story:** As a student, I want my profile to comprehensively track my academic progress, course completions, grades, and skill development, so that I can see my growth and demonstrate my competencies to mentors and employers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN courses are completed THEN the system SHALL record grades, ScrollXP earned, and competency achievements
2. WHEN skills are developed THEN the system SHALL track technical skills, spiritual gifts, and practical abilities with proficiency levels
3. WHEN assessments are taken THEN the system SHALL record scores, attempts, and improvement trajectories
4. WHEN projects are completed THEN the system SHALL link to portfolios, code repositories, and practical outputs
5. WHEN milestones are reached THEN the system SHALL celebrate achievements and update progress dashboards
6. WHEN transcripts are needed THEN the system SHALL generate comprehensive academic records with ScrollXP and traditional metrics

### Requirement 3: Spiritual Formation and Prophetic Development Tracking

**User Story:** As a ScrollMentor, I want to track each student's spiritual formation, prophetic development, and character growth, so that I can provide appropriate guidance and confirm their readiness for kingdom assignments.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN spiritual assessments occur THEN the system SHALL track prophetic accuracy, spiritual discernment, and biblical knowledge
2. WHEN character development is measured THEN the system SHALL record integrity, humility, faithfulness, and servant leadership indicators
3. WHEN prophetic confirmations are received THEN the system SHALL document mentor testimonies, prophetic words, and divine confirmations
4. WHEN spiritual disciplines are practiced THEN the system SHALL track prayer consistency, scripture study, and worship engagement
5. WHEN ministry involvement occurs THEN the system SHALL record service activities, leadership roles, and kingdom impact
6. WHEN spiritual maturity is assessed THEN the system SHALL provide comprehensive spiritual formation reports for mentors and leaders

### Requirement 4: Portfolio and Project Showcase System

**User Story:** As a student, I want my profile to showcase my best work, projects, and achievements in a professional portfolio format, so that employers and collaborators can see my practical capabilities and kingdom impact.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN projects are completed THEN the system SHALL allow students to add them to their portfolio with descriptions, links, and impact metrics
2. WHEN portfolios are curated THEN the system SHALL support multiple portfolio views (technical, ministry, academic, professional)
3. WHEN work samples are displayed THEN the system SHALL support code repositories, design files, written works, and multimedia content
4. WHEN achievements are highlighted THEN the system SHALL showcase awards, recognitions, and significant accomplishments
5. WHEN portfolio sharing occurs THEN the system SHALL provide controlled access for employers, mentors, and collaboration partners
6. WHEN portfolio updates happen THEN the system SHALL maintain version history and allow continuous improvement

### Requirement 5: Mentorship and Relationship Management System

**User Story:** As a student, I want my profile to track my relationships with mentors, peers, and faculty, so that I can maintain meaningful connections and receive appropriate guidance throughout my journey.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN mentors are assigned THEN the system SHALL track mentor relationships, meeting history, and guidance received
2. WHEN peer connections are made THEN the system SHALL facilitate study groups, project teams, and accountability partnerships
3. WHEN faculty interactions occur THEN the system SHALL record course instructors, feedback received, and academic relationships
4. WHEN networking happens THEN the system SHALL track professional connections, alumni relationships, and industry contacts
5. WHEN relationship health is assessed THEN the system SHALL monitor engagement levels and suggest relationship strengthening activities
6. WHEN mentorship outcomes are measured THEN the system SHALL track growth attributed to specific mentoring relationships

### Requirement 6: Personalized Learning Path and Recommendation Engine

**User Story:** As a student, I want my profile to generate personalized learning recommendations and optimal course sequences, so that I can efficiently progress toward my calling and career goals.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN learning paths are generated THEN the system SHALL create personalized course sequences based on calling, interests, and career goals
2. WHEN recommendations are made THEN the system SHALL suggest courses, projects, mentors, and opportunities aligned with student goals
3. WHEN progress is analyzed THEN the system SHALL identify learning gaps and recommend remediation or advancement opportunities
4. WHEN career alignment is assessed THEN the system SHALL suggest internships, projects, and experiences that advance career objectives
5. WHEN spiritual development is guided THEN the system SHALL recommend spiritual disciplines, ministry opportunities, and prophetic training
6. WHEN path adjustments are needed THEN the system SHALL adapt recommendations based on changing interests and divine direction

### Requirement 7: Global Community and Cultural Integration System

**User Story:** As an international student, I want my profile to reflect my cultural background and connect me with global community members, so that I can learn in culturally relevant ways while building international relationships.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN cultural background is captured THEN the system SHALL record nationality, languages, cultural context, and regional challenges
2. WHEN cultural adaptation occurs THEN the system SHALL provide culturally relevant examples, case studies, and learning materials
3. WHEN global connections are made THEN the system SHALL connect students with peers from similar or complementary cultural backgrounds
4. WHEN language support is needed THEN the system SHALL provide multilingual resources and translation assistance
5. WHEN cultural competency is developed THEN the system SHALL track cross-cultural learning and global perspective development
6. WHEN international collaboration occurs THEN the system SHALL facilitate global project teams and cultural exchange opportunities

### Requirement 8: Privacy and Access Control Management System

**User Story:** As a student, I want granular control over who can access different parts of my profile, so that I can maintain appropriate privacy while sharing relevant information with authorized parties.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN privacy settings are configured THEN the system SHALL allow granular control over profile visibility and access permissions
2. WHEN information is shared THEN the system SHALL support different access levels for mentors, peers, employers, and public viewing
3. WHEN sensitive data is protected THEN the system SHALL secure spiritual formation records, personal information, and private communications
4. WHEN access is granted THEN the system SHALL log all profile access and provide transparency about who viewed what information
5. WHEN consent is managed THEN the system SHALL require explicit consent for sharing profile data with external parties
6. WHEN data portability is needed THEN the system SHALL allow students to export their complete profile data in standard formats

### Requirement 9: Integration with ScrollUniversity Ecosystem

**User Story:** As a system architect, I want student profiles to integrate seamlessly with all ScrollUniversity systems, so that student data flows efficiently across the platform while maintaining consistency and accuracy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN course enrollment occurs THEN the system SHALL sync with scroll-course-spec to track progress and achievements
2. WHEN projects are managed THEN the system SHALL integrate with scroll-projects-spec to showcase student work and impact
3. WHEN gamification is applied THEN the system SHALL sync with scroll-gamified-learning to track XP, badges, and achievements
4. WHEN certifications are earned THEN the system SHALL integrate with scroll-seal-certification to display credentials and verification
5. WHEN AI tutoring is accessed THEN the system SHALL sync with scroll-faculty-ai to personalize tutoring based on profile data
6. WHEN platform interactions occur THEN the system SHALL log all activities through scroll-audit-trail-spec for comprehensive tracking

### Requirement 10: Analytics and Insights Dashboard System

**User Story:** As a student and mentor, I want comprehensive analytics and insights about learning progress, spiritual development, and goal achievement, so that I can make informed decisions about educational and spiritual growth.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN analytics are generated THEN the system SHALL provide visual dashboards showing academic progress, spiritual growth, and skill development
2. WHEN insights are provided THEN the system SHALL identify patterns, trends, and opportunities for improvement or advancement
3. WHEN goal tracking occurs THEN the system SHALL monitor progress toward academic, spiritual, and career objectives with milestone celebrations
4. WHEN performance analysis happens THEN the system SHALL compare individual progress with peer groups and provide benchmarking insights
5. WHEN predictive analytics are applied THEN the system SHALL forecast likely outcomes and suggest interventions for optimal success
6. WHEN reporting is needed THEN the system SHALL generate comprehensive reports for students, mentors, and administrators
