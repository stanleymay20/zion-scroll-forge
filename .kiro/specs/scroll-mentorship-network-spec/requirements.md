# ScrollUniversity Mentorship Network System Requirements

## Introduction

The Mentorship Network System bridges the gap between AI-powered education and human wisdom transfer, creating meaningful discipleship relationships that guide students through their academic journey and into their kingdom calling. This system facilitates mentor-student matching, relationship management, discipleship pathway tracking, and real-world ministry placement coordination.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Mentor Profile and Matching System

**User Story:** As a potential mentor, I want to create a comprehensive profile of my expertise and calling so that I can be matched with students who would benefit from my guidance and experience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a mentor creates a profile THEN the system SHALL capture their expertise areas, ministry experience, availability, and mentoring capacity
2. WHEN mentor profiles are complete THEN the system SHALL validate their credentials, testimonials, and spiritual maturity
3. WHEN students need mentorship THEN the system SHALL use intelligent matching algorithms to suggest optimal mentor-student pairings
4. WHEN matching occurs THEN the system SHALL consider academic focus, ministry calling, personality compatibility, and geographical proximity
5. WHEN matches are proposed THEN the system SHALL require mutual consent from both mentor and student before establishing relationships
6. WHEN mentor capacity is reached THEN the system SHALL automatically adjust availability and suggest alternative mentors

### Requirement 2: Student Mentorship Request and Onboarding

**User Story:** As a student, I want to request mentorship in specific areas of my development so that I can receive personalized guidance from experienced kingdom leaders.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN students request mentorship THEN the system SHALL assess their current academic progress, spiritual maturity, and specific needs
2. WHEN mentorship requests are submitted THEN the system SHALL identify the type of mentorship needed (academic, spiritual, ministry, career)
3. WHEN students are matched with mentors THEN the system SHALL facilitate introduction processes and relationship establishment
4. WHEN mentorship begins THEN the system SHALL provide onboarding resources and relationship guidelines for both parties
5. WHEN students have multiple mentors THEN the system SHALL coordinate between mentors to avoid conflicts and ensure comprehensive coverage
6. WHEN mentorship needs change THEN the system SHALL allow for mentor transitions and relationship adjustments

### Requirement 3: Discipleship Pathway Tracking and Management

**User Story:** As a mentor, I want to track my mentee's discipleship progress and spiritual development so that I can provide targeted guidance and celebrate growth milestones.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN discipleship relationships begin THEN the system SHALL establish personalized discipleship pathways based on student calling and maturity
2. WHEN discipleship activities occur THEN the system SHALL track meetings, assignments, spiritual exercises, and breakthrough moments
3. WHEN spiritual growth is observed THEN the system SHALL document character development, spiritual gifts emergence, and ministry readiness
4. WHEN discipleship milestones are reached THEN the system SHALL trigger celebrations, advancement opportunities, and expanded responsibilities
5. WHEN discipleship challenges arise THEN the system SHALL provide resources, additional support, and intervention strategies
6. WHEN discipleship pathways are completed THEN the system SHALL transition students to peer mentoring or leadership development roles

### Requirement 4: Cross-Generational Wisdom Transfer System

**User Story:** As a senior leader, I want to systematically transfer my knowledge and experience to the next generation so that kingdom wisdom is preserved and multiplied.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN senior leaders join the network THEN the system SHALL capture their life experiences, ministry lessons, and wisdom insights
2. WHEN wisdom transfer occurs THEN the system SHALL facilitate structured knowledge sharing through stories, case studies, and practical applications
3. WHEN generational gaps exist THEN the system SHALL bridge communication styles and cultural differences between mentors and students
4. WHEN wisdom is shared THEN the system SHALL document key insights, lessons learned, and practical applications for future reference
5. WHEN wisdom transfer is successful THEN the system SHALL measure impact through student application and ministry effectiveness
6. WHEN legacy building occurs THEN the system SHALL create permanent records of wisdom transfer for institutional memory

### Requirement 5: Ministry Placement and Opportunity Coordination

**User Story:** As a ministry leader, I want to identify and place qualified students in ministry opportunities so that they can apply their education in real-world kingdom service.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN ministry opportunities arise THEN the system SHALL match student skills, calling, and readiness with available positions
2. WHEN placements are considered THEN the system SHALL verify student qualifications, mentor recommendations, and spiritual maturity
3. WHEN ministry placements occur THEN the system SHALL coordinate between educational requirements and ministry responsibilities
4. WHEN students serve in ministry THEN the system SHALL track their performance, growth, and impact in real-world applications
5. WHEN ministry challenges arise THEN the system SHALL provide ongoing mentorship support and problem-solving assistance
6. WHEN ministry seasons end THEN the system SHALL evaluate outcomes, document lessons learned, and plan next steps

### Requirement 6: Mentorship Relationship Management and Communication

**User Story:** As a participant in mentorship relationships, I want tools to manage communication, schedule meetings, and track relationship progress so that mentorship is effective and sustainable.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN mentorship relationships are active THEN the system SHALL provide communication tools for messaging, video calls, and document sharing
2. WHEN meetings are scheduled THEN the system SHALL coordinate calendars, send reminders, and provide meeting preparation resources
3. WHEN relationship progress is tracked THEN the system SHALL document meeting notes, action items, and development milestones
4. WHEN relationship challenges occur THEN the system SHALL provide conflict resolution resources and mediation support
5. WHEN relationships need adjustment THEN the system SHALL facilitate transitions, mentor changes, or relationship restructuring
6. WHEN relationships conclude THEN the system SHALL conduct evaluations, document outcomes, and maintain ongoing connections

### Requirement 7: Group Mentorship and Cohort Management

**User Story:** As a mentor with capacity for multiple students, I want to facilitate group mentorship experiences so that students can learn from each other while receiving my guidance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN group mentorship is established THEN the system SHALL form cohorts based on complementary learning needs and compatible personalities
2. WHEN group sessions occur THEN the system SHALL provide tools for group communication, shared resources, and collaborative learning
3. WHEN peer learning happens THEN the system SHALL facilitate student-to-student mentorship and knowledge sharing
4. WHEN group dynamics develop THEN the system SHALL monitor group health, address conflicts, and optimize group composition
5. WHEN individual needs arise THEN the system SHALL balance group activities with personalized one-on-one mentorship
6. WHEN cohorts graduate THEN the system SHALL maintain alumni networks and ongoing peer support systems

### Requirement 8: Mentorship Quality Assurance and Effectiveness Measurement

**User Story:** As a mentorship coordinator, I want to ensure high-quality mentorship experiences and measure their effectiveness so that the program continuously improves and produces kingdom impact.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN mentorship relationships are active THEN the system SHALL monitor relationship health, engagement levels, and progress indicators
2. WHEN quality issues arise THEN the system SHALL identify problems early and provide intervention resources and support
3. WHEN mentorship effectiveness is measured THEN the system SHALL track student outcomes, spiritual growth, and ministry readiness
4. WHEN feedback is collected THEN the system SHALL gather input from both mentors and students to improve the program
5. WHEN mentorship standards are maintained THEN the system SHALL ensure mentors meet ongoing requirements and development needs
6. WHEN program improvements are needed THEN the system SHALL analyze data, identify trends, and recommend enhancements

### Requirement 9: Global Mentorship Network and Cultural Adaptation

**User Story:** As a global student, I want access to mentors who understand my cultural context and can guide me in my specific geographical and cultural ministry setting.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN global students seek mentorship THEN the system SHALL connect them with mentors who understand their cultural and geographical context
2. WHEN cross-cultural mentorship occurs THEN the system SHALL provide cultural sensitivity training and communication guidelines
3. WHEN language barriers exist THEN the system SHALL offer translation services and multilingual mentorship resources
4. WHEN time zone differences impact relationships THEN the system SHALL coordinate scheduling and provide asynchronous communication tools
5. WHEN cultural ministry contexts vary THEN the system SHALL adapt mentorship approaches to local ministry needs and opportunities
6. WHEN global expansion occurs THEN the system SHALL recruit and train mentors from diverse cultural backgrounds and ministry contexts

### Requirement 10: Integration with Academic and Spiritual Development Systems

**User Story:** As an academic advisor, I want mentorship activities to integrate with academic progress and spiritual development so that students receive holistic guidance aligned with their educational journey.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN students progress academically THEN the system SHALL coordinate mentorship focus with current coursework and learning objectives
2. WHEN spiritual development occurs THEN the system SHALL align mentorship activities with spiritual formation goals and assessments
3. WHEN academic challenges arise THEN the system SHALL mobilize mentorship support for study strategies, motivation, and problem-solving
4. WHEN spiritual crises occur THEN the system SHALL provide immediate mentorship intervention and spiritual care resources
5. WHEN degree milestones are reached THEN the system SHALL celebrate achievements and adjust mentorship focus for next phases
6. WHEN graduation approaches THEN the system SHALL transition mentorship toward career preparation and ministry deployment
