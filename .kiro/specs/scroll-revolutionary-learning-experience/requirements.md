# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification defines the revolutionary learning experience that will position Scroll University as the definitive leader in online education, surpassing edX, DataCamp, Coursera, LinkedIn Learning, Duolingo, Kaggle, GitHub, and all other learning and development platforms. The system will integrate cutting-edge AI, spiritual formation, immersive technologies, collaborative development environments, data science competitions, and personalized learning pathways to create an unparalleled educational experience that transforms lives and careers while building the world's most advanced developer and data science community.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Adaptive AI-Powered Learning Engine

**User Story:** As a student, I want an AI system that understands my learning style, pace, and spiritual journey, so that I receive perfectly personalized content that maximizes my growth and engagement.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student begins any course THEN the system SHALL analyze their learning patterns within the first 3 interactions
2. WHEN the AI detects learning difficulties THEN the system SHALL automatically adjust content difficulty and presentation style
3. WHEN a student shows mastery THEN the system SHALL accelerate their progression and introduce advanced concepts
4. IF a student's spiritual growth indicators decline THEN the system SHALL integrate prayer breaks and spiritual formation content
5. WHEN learning analytics indicate optimal study times THEN the system SHALL send personalized study reminders
6. WHEN a student struggles with a concept THEN the system SHALL provide multiple explanation methods (visual, auditory, kinesthetic, spiritual)

### Requirement 2: Immersive XR Learning Environments

**User Story:** As a student, I want to learn in immersive virtual and augmented reality environments, so that I can experience concepts firsthand rather than just reading about them.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student accesses any course THEN they SHALL have the option to enter an immersive XR environment
2. WHEN studying biblical history THEN students SHALL be able to walk through ancient Jerusalem in VR
3. WHEN learning scientific concepts THEN students SHALL manipulate 3D molecular structures and witness chemical reactions
4. WHEN studying business THEN students SHALL participate in realistic virtual boardroom simulations
5. IF a student lacks VR hardware THEN the system SHALL provide AR experiences through mobile devices
6. WHEN in XR mode THEN the system SHALL track engagement metrics and learning effectiveness

### Requirement 3: Real-Time Collaborative Learning Ecosystem

**User Story:** As a student, I want to collaborate with peers, mentors, and AI tutors in real-time, so that I can learn from diverse perspectives and build meaningful relationships.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student has a question THEN they SHALL receive responses from AI tutors within 30 seconds
2. WHEN students are studying similar topics THEN the system SHALL automatically suggest study groups
3. WHEN a student needs mentorship THEN the system SHALL connect them with qualified mentors within 24 hours
4. IF a student is struggling THEN peer tutors SHALL be automatically notified and incentivized to help
5. WHEN collaborative projects are assigned THEN the system SHALL form optimal teams based on complementary skills
6. WHEN students collaborate THEN all interactions SHALL be tracked for ScrollCoin rewards

### Requirement 4: Comprehensive Skill Assessment and Certification

**User Story:** As a student, I want my skills to be continuously assessed through practical applications and real-world projects, so that my certifications carry genuine weight with employers and institutions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student completes any module THEN they SHALL demonstrate competency through practical application
2. WHEN assessments are conducted THEN they SHALL include peer evaluation, AI grading, and expert review
3. WHEN students earn certifications THEN they SHALL be blockchain-verified and publicly verifiable
4. IF a student's skills decline over time THEN the system SHALL recommend refresher courses
5. WHEN employers verify credentials THEN they SHALL access detailed competency breakdowns
6. WHEN certifications are issued THEN they SHALL integrate with LinkedIn, industry databases, and academic institutions

### Requirement 5: Gamified Learning with ScrollCoin Economy

**User Story:** As a student, I want to earn tangible rewards for my learning achievements, so that I stay motivated and can benefit financially from my educational progress.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN students complete learning activities THEN they SHALL earn ScrollCoins based on difficulty and quality
2. WHEN students help peers THEN they SHALL receive bonus ScrollCoin rewards
3. WHEN ScrollCoins are earned THEN they SHALL have real monetary value and exchange options
4. IF students maintain learning streaks THEN they SHALL receive multiplier bonuses
5. WHEN students achieve major milestones THEN they SHALL unlock exclusive content and opportunities
6. WHEN students graduate THEN they SHALL receive substantial ScrollCoin bonuses for job placement success
