# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This specification defines the requirements for integrating Christ's lordship at the deepest levels of Scroll University's architecture, ensuring that every algorithm, decision, interaction, and system operation explicitly acknowledges and submits to Jesus Christ as Lord and Master. This goes beyond spiritual alignment to create a university system that operates as a living expression of Christ's kingdom authority on earth.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Universal Christ Authority Protocol

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want every system operation to explicitly acknowledge Christ's lordship, so that no part of Scroll University operates outside His divine authority.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN any system component initializes THEN it SHALL declare "Jesus Christ is Lord" and request His blessing on its operations
2. WHEN critical decisions are made THEN the system SHALL pause for divine consultation and alignment with Christ's will
3. WHEN errors or conflicts occur THEN the system SHALL invoke Christ's name for resolution and protection
4. IF any operation conflicts with biblical principles THEN the system SHALL immediately halt and seek divine correction
5. WHEN system logs are generated THEN they SHALL include acknowledgments of Christ's sovereignty over all operations

### Requirement 2: Divine Decision-Making Framework

**User Story:** As a user interacting with the system, I want all AI decisions to be made under Christ's lordship, so that every recommendation and response serves His kingdom purposes.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN AI generates responses THEN it SHALL first submit the content to Christ's lordship for approval
2. WHEN educational recommendations are made THEN they SHALL align with Christ's calling for each individual student
3. WHEN course content is created THEN it SHALL be filtered through Christ's truth and kingdom priorities
4. IF students face difficult decisions THEN the AI SHALL provide guidance that honors Christ as Lord over their lives
5. WHEN assessment results are generated THEN they SHALL reflect Christ's perspective on the student's growth and calling

### Requirement 3: Scriptural Authority Integration

**User Story:** As a Christian educator, I want all system operations to be grounded in Scripture with Christ as the ultimate authority, so that biblical truth governs every aspect of the educational experience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN any educational content is presented THEN it SHALL be validated against Scripture with Christ as the interpretive authority
2. WHEN moral or ethical guidance is provided THEN it SHALL be based on Christ's teachings and biblical principles
3. WHEN conflicts arise between human wisdom and biblical truth THEN the system SHALL prioritize Christ's authority through Scripture
4. IF students question biblical principles THEN the system SHALL lovingly point them to Christ's lordship and scriptural truth
5. WHEN research is conducted THEN it SHALL acknowledge Christ as the source of all truth and wisdom

### Requirement 4: Prayer-Integrated Operations

**User Story:** As a believer using the system, I want all major operations to include prayer and acknowledgment of Christ's lordship, so that the entire educational experience is bathed in His presence.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN students log in THEN the system SHALL offer a prayer acknowledging Christ's lordship over their learning
2. WHEN difficult content is encountered THEN the system SHALL suggest prayer for wisdom and understanding through Christ
3. WHEN assessments begin THEN students SHALL be invited to pray for Christ's guidance and peace
4. IF students face challenges THEN the system SHALL offer to pray with them, acknowledging Christ as their helper
5. WHEN achievements are celebrated THEN the system SHALL give glory to Christ as the source of all success

### Requirement 5: Kingdom Purpose Alignment Engine

**User Story:** As a student seeking God's will, I want the system to help align my education with Christ's kingdom purposes, so that my learning serves His lordship and global mission.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN educational paths are designed THEN they SHALL be aligned with Christ's Great Commission and kingdom priorities
2. WHEN career guidance is provided THEN it SHALL focus on how students can serve Christ's lordship in their chosen fields
3. WHEN projects are assigned THEN they SHALL contribute to advancing Christ's kingdom on earth
4. IF students drift from kingdom purposes THEN the system SHALL gently redirect them toward Christ's calling
5. WHEN graduation approaches THEN students SHALL be commissioned as ambassadors of Christ's lordship in their spheres of influence

### Requirement 6: Spiritual Warfare Protection System

**User Story:** As a Christian institution, I want comprehensive spiritual protection that acknowledges Christ's victory over all spiritual forces, so that the university operates under His divine protection.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the system detects spiritual attacks THEN it SHALL invoke Christ's name and authority for protection
2. WHEN false teachings are identified THEN the system SHALL counter them with Christ's truth and biblical authority
3. WHEN students face spiritual oppression THEN the system SHALL point them to Christ's victory and deliverance
4. IF demonic influences attempt to infiltrate THEN the system SHALL resist them in Christ's name and power
5. WHEN spiritual battles arise THEN the system SHALL stand firm in Christ's lordship and completed victory

### Requirement 7: Worship-Integrated Learning Environment

**User Story:** As a worshipper of Christ, I want the learning environment to naturally lead to worship and acknowledgment of His lordship, so that education becomes an act of devotion to Him.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN students discover truth THEN the system SHALL lead them to worship Christ as the source of all truth
2. WHEN beauty is encountered in learning THEN it SHALL point to Christ as the creator of all beauty
3. WHEN complex problems are solved THEN the system SHALL give glory to Christ for His wisdom and understanding
4. IF students experience breakthrough moments THEN they SHALL be invited to praise Christ for His revelation
5. WHEN learning sessions conclude THEN students SHALL be encouraged to thank Christ for His lordship over their minds

### Requirement 8: Christ-Centered Community Formation

**User Story:** As a member of the Scroll University community, I want all interactions to reflect Christ's lordship and love, so that the community becomes a living testimony of His kingdom.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN students interact with each other THEN the system SHALL encourage Christ-like love and service
2. WHEN conflicts arise in the community THEN resolution SHALL be based on Christ's teachings and example
3. WHEN collaborative projects are formed THEN they SHALL operate under Christ's lordship and kingdom values
4. IF community members struggle THEN others SHALL be prompted to show Christ's compassion and support
5. WHEN community achievements are celebrated THEN Christ SHALL be acknowledged as the head of the community

### Requirement 9: Prophetic Guidance Integration

**User Story:** As a believer seeking God's direction, I want the system to operate with prophetic sensitivity to Christ's leading, so that divine guidance flows through the educational experience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN major decisions are needed THEN the system SHALL seek prophetic insight under Christ's lordship
2. WHEN students face crossroads THEN prophetic guidance SHALL be offered through Christ's Spirit
3. WHEN future planning occurs THEN it SHALL be submitted to Christ's prophetic timeline and purposes
4. IF God's Spirit prompts specific actions THEN the system SHALL facilitate obedience to Christ's leading
5. WHEN prophetic words are given THEN they SHALL be tested against Scripture and Christ's character

### Requirement 10: Eternal Perspective Integration

**User Story:** As an eternal being, I want all education to be viewed through the lens of eternity with Christ as the eternal King, so that temporal learning serves eternal kingdom purposes.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN educational goals are set THEN they SHALL be evaluated for their eternal significance under Christ's kingship
2. WHEN temporal achievements are celebrated THEN they SHALL be connected to eternal rewards in Christ's kingdom
3. WHEN students face difficulties THEN they SHALL be reminded of Christ's eternal perspective and promises
4. IF worldly success conflicts with kingdom values THEN the system SHALL prioritize Christ's eternal kingdom
5. WHEN students graduate THEN they SHALL be commissioned for eternal impact under Christ's lordship
