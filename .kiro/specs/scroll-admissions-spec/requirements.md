# ScrollAdmissionsSpec Requirements Document

## Introduction

The ScrollAdmissionsSpec creates a comprehensive admissions system that goes beyond traditional academic credentials to assess the complete person - their calling, character, spiritual readiness, and kingdom potential. Operating under the principle "Many are called, but few are chosen" (Matthew 22:14), this system identifies and admits students who are not only academically capable but spiritually prepared and divinely called to participate in scroll-aligned education. The admissions process integrates academic assessment, spiritual discernment, character evaluation, and prophetic confirmation to ensure that each admitted student is positioned for success and kingdom impact.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Holistic Application and Assessment System

**User Story:** As a prospective student, I want to submit a comprehensive application that captures my academic background, spiritual journey, character development, and kingdom calling, so that ScrollUniversity can assess my readiness for scroll-aligned education.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN applications are submitted THEN the system SHALL collect academic transcripts, spiritual formation history, character references, and calling testimony
2. WHEN application review occurs THEN the system SHALL evaluate academic preparedness, spiritual maturity, character development, and kingdom alignment
3. WHEN assessment is conducted THEN the system SHALL use multiple evaluation methods including written responses, interviews, and prophetic discernment
4. WHEN application completeness is verified THEN the system SHALL ensure all required components are submitted before proceeding to review
5. WHEN application tracking is provided THEN the system SHALL give applicants visibility into their application status and next steps
6. WHEN application deadlines are managed THEN the system SHALL enforce submission deadlines while providing grace for exceptional circumstances

### Requirement 2: Academic Readiness and Prerequisite Assessment System

**User Story:** As an admissions officer, I want to assess each applicant's academic readiness and prerequisite knowledge, so that I can ensure they are prepared for the rigor of scroll-aligned education.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN academic assessment occurs THEN the system SHALL evaluate prior education, standardized test scores, and demonstrated academic competencies
2. WHEN prerequisite evaluation is conducted THEN the system SHALL assess foundational knowledge in mathematics, science, language arts, and critical thinking
3. WHEN academic gaps are identified THEN the system SHALL recommend preparatory courses or bridge programs to address deficiencies
4. WHEN academic potential is measured THEN the system SHALL consider not just past performance but capacity for growth and learning
5. WHEN alternative credentials are presented THEN the system SHALL evaluate non-traditional educational backgrounds and life experience
6. WHEN academic accommodation is needed THEN the system SHALL assess learning differences and provide appropriate support recommendations

### Requirement 3: Spiritual Formation and Calling Assessment System

**User Story:** As a spiritual leader, I want to assess each applicant's spiritual maturity and divine calling, so that I can confirm their readiness for scroll-aligned education and kingdom assignment.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN spiritual assessment occurs THEN the system SHALL evaluate salvation testimony, spiritual disciplines, and evidence of spiritual growth
2. WHEN calling evaluation is conducted THEN the system SHALL assess clarity of divine purpose, ministry experience, and kingdom vision
3. WHEN spiritual maturity is measured THEN the system SHALL consider prayer life, biblical knowledge, and demonstration of spiritual fruit
4. WHEN prophetic confirmation is sought THEN the system SHALL integrate prophetic input and spiritual discernment into the admissions process
5. WHEN spiritual readiness is determined THEN the system SHALL assess preparedness for spiritual formation and prophetic training
6. WHEN spiritual development needs are identified THEN the system SHALL recommend spiritual preparation activities and mentorship

### Requirement 4: Character and Integrity Evaluation System

**User Story:** As a character assessor, I want to evaluate each applicant's integrity, character development, and moral foundation, so that I can ensure they will contribute positively to the ScrollUniversity community.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN character assessment occurs THEN the system SHALL evaluate integrity, honesty, humility, and servant leadership qualities
2. WHEN reference verification is conducted THEN the system SHALL contact character references and validate their testimonies
3. WHEN background checks are performed THEN the system SHALL conduct appropriate screening while respecting privacy and redemption
4. WHEN character development is assessed THEN the system SHALL consider growth trajectory and response to challenges
5. WHEN community fit is evaluated THEN the system SHALL assess ability to contribute positively to the ScrollUniversity community
6. WHEN character concerns are identified THEN the system SHALL provide pathways for character development and restoration

### Requirement 5: Financial Readiness and Scholarship Assessment System

**User Story:** As a financial aid administrator, I want to assess each applicant's financial situation and scholarship eligibility, so that I can ensure financial barriers don't prevent qualified students from accessing scroll-aligned education.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN financial assessment occurs THEN the system SHALL evaluate family income, financial need, and ability to fund education
2. WHEN scholarship eligibility is determined THEN the system SHALL assess merit, need, and kingdom calling for scholarship awards
3. WHEN financial planning is provided THEN the system SHALL help families understand costs and develop funding strategies
4. WHEN ScrollCoin opportunities are presented THEN the system SHALL explain how students can earn coins through learning and achievement
5. WHEN financial barriers are identified THEN the system SHALL provide alternative funding options and payment plans
6. WHEN financial stewardship is assessed THEN the system SHALL evaluate responsible money management and kingdom financial principles

### Requirement 6: Interview and Personal Interaction System

**User Story:** As an interviewer, I want to conduct comprehensive interviews with applicants, so that I can assess their communication skills, passion for learning, and alignment with ScrollUniversity values.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN interviews are scheduled THEN the system SHALL coordinate availability between applicants and interview panels
2. WHEN interviews are conducted THEN the system SHALL assess communication skills, passion for learning, and value alignment
3. WHEN interview evaluation occurs THEN the system SHALL use structured rubrics to ensure fair and consistent assessment
4. WHEN virtual interviews are needed THEN the system SHALL provide technology platforms for remote interview capabilities
5. WHEN interview feedback is collected THEN the system SHALL gather input from multiple interviewers for comprehensive evaluation
6. WHEN interview outcomes are recorded THEN the system SHALL document assessments and recommendations for admissions decisions

### Requirement 7: Admissions Decision and Notification System

**User Story:** As an admissions committee member, I want a systematic process for making admissions decisions and communicating outcomes, so that decisions are fair, well-documented, and clearly communicated to applicants.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN admissions decisions are made THEN the system SHALL integrate all assessment components into comprehensive applicant profiles
2. WHEN committee review occurs THEN the system SHALL facilitate collaborative decision-making with proper documentation
3. WHEN decisions are finalized THEN the system SHALL ensure all decisions are reviewed and approved by appropriate authorities
4. WHEN notifications are sent THEN the system SHALL communicate decisions clearly with next steps and deadlines
5. WHEN appeals are submitted THEN the system SHALL provide fair appeal processes for applicants who wish to contest decisions
6. WHEN decision analytics are generated THEN the system SHALL track admission rates, demographics, and outcome patterns

### Requirement 8: Enrollment Confirmation and Onboarding System

**User Story:** As an admitted student, I want a clear enrollment process and comprehensive onboarding, so that I can confirm my attendance and prepare effectively for my ScrollUniversity experience.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN enrollment confirmation is required THEN the system SHALL provide clear deadlines and requirements for securing admission
2. WHEN deposits are processed THEN the system SHALL handle enrollment deposits and payment processing securely
3. WHEN onboarding is initiated THEN the system SHALL provide comprehensive orientation materials and preparation resources
4. WHEN course registration occurs THEN the system SHALL integrate with course systems for initial course selection and scheduling
5. WHEN mentor assignment happens THEN the system SHALL match new students with appropriate ScrollMentors for guidance
6. WHEN community integration is facilitated THEN the system SHALL connect new students with peers and community resources

### Requirement 9: Waitlist and Deferral Management System

**User Story:** As an admissions administrator, I want to manage waitlists and deferrals effectively, so that I can optimize enrollment while providing fair opportunities for qualified applicants.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN waitlist management occurs THEN the system SHALL maintain ranked waitlists with clear communication about status and likelihood
2. WHEN deferral requests are submitted THEN the system SHALL process requests for delayed enrollment with appropriate approval workflows
3. WHEN spots become available THEN the system SHALL notify waitlisted applicants promptly with response deadlines
4. WHEN waitlist movement is tracked THEN the system SHALL provide analytics on waitlist conversion rates and patterns
5. WHEN deferral conditions are managed THEN the system SHALL track deferred students and facilitate their eventual enrollment
6. WHEN capacity planning is conducted THEN the system SHALL help optimize enrollment numbers and waitlist management

### Requirement 10: Integration with ScrollUniversity Ecosystem and Analytics

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want the admissions system to integrate seamlessly with all ScrollUniversity systems while providing comprehensive analytics on admissions effectiveness and student outcomes.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN system integration occurs THEN the admissions system SHALL connect with student-profile-spec, course-spec, and other relevant components
2. WHEN data synchronization happens THEN the system SHALL maintain consistency across all ScrollUniversity systems
3. WHEN analytics are generated THEN the system SHALL provide insights on admissions effectiveness, student success rates, and program outcomes
4. WHEN predictive modeling is applied THEN the system SHALL use historical data to improve admissions decisions and student success prediction
5. WHEN continuous improvement occurs THEN the system SHALL use outcome data to refine admissions criteria and processes
6. WHEN reporting is provided THEN the system SHALL generate comprehensive reports for administrators, faculty, and stakeholders on admissions performance and student success
