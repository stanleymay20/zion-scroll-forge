# AI Automation Expansion Requirements
**"Whatever is true, whatever is noble, whatever is right... think about such things" - Philippians 4:8**

## Introduction

This specification identifies opportunities to leverage AI tools (beyond Zapier) to automate ScrollUniversity operations, enhance educational quality, and reduce operational costs. While Zapier handles workflow automation, AI can handle intelligent tasks requiring understanding, analysis, and decision-making.

## Glossary

- **AI Agent**: Autonomous AI system that can perform tasks with minimal human supervision
- **LLM**: Large Language Model (GPT-4, Claude, etc.)
- **RAG**: Retrieval-Augmented Generation - AI that references specific documents
- **Vector Database**: Database optimized for semantic search and AI retrieval
- **Embedding**: Numerical representation of text for semantic similarity
- **Fine-tuning**: Training AI model on specific data for specialized tasks
- **Prompt Engineering**: Crafting instructions to get optimal AI responses
- **Token**: Unit of text processed by AI (roughly 0.75 words)
- **ScrollDean**: ScrollUniversity's AI tutor system
- **Academic Integrity System**: Multi-layered system preventing and detecting cheating

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Intelligent Content Creation & Curation

**User Story:** As a faculty member, I want AI to help create world-class course content so that I can focus on teaching and mentoring rather than content production.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1.1 WHEN a faculty member provides a course outline and learning objectives, THE Content Creation AI SHALL generate comprehensive lecture materials including explanations, examples, case studies, and discussion questions that meet world-class academic standards

1.2 WHEN generating course content, THE Content Creation AI SHALL integrate biblical and theological perspectives naturally throughout the material without compromising academic rigor

1.3 WHEN creating assessments, THE Assessment Generation AI SHALL produce unique problem sets, essay questions, and project specifications for each student to prevent cheating

1.4 WHEN curating resources, THE Resource Curation AI SHALL identify and recommend peer-reviewed papers, textbooks, videos, and case studies relevant to specific learning objectives

1.5 WHEN faculty reviews AI-generated content, THE System SHALL provide editing interface allowing human oversight and refinement before publication

### Requirement 2: Automated Grading & Feedback

**User Story:** As an instructor, I want AI to grade assignments and provide detailed feedback so that students receive immediate, personalized responses while I focus on complex evaluations.

#### Acceptance Criteria

2.1 WHEN a student submits a programming assignment, THE Code Grading AI SHALL evaluate correctness, efficiency, style, documentation, and best practices, providing line-by-line feedback

2.2 WHEN a student submits an essay, THE Essay Grading AI SHALL assess thesis clarity, argument structure, evidence quality, writing quality, and citation accuracy, providing paragraph-level feedback

2.3 WHEN grading mathematical work, THE Math Grading AI SHALL evaluate both final answers and solution methodology, identifying conceptual errors and providing hints for improvement

2.4 WHEN providing feedback, THE Grading AI SHALL maintain world-class standards equivalent to Harvard/MIT faculty expectations

2.5 WHEN AI confidence is below 85%, THE System SHALL flag submission for human review rather than providing automated grade

### Requirement 3: Intelligent Academic Integrity Monitoring

**User Story:** As an academic integrity officer, I want AI to detect sophisticated cheating attempts so that we maintain the highest standards of honesty while minimizing false positives.

#### Acceptance Criteria

3.1 WHEN a student submits written work, THE Plagiarism Detection AI SHALL compare against 70+ billion web pages, academic databases, and previous submissions, generating detailed similarity reports

3.2 WHEN analyzing submissions, THE AI Content Detector SHALL identify text generated by ChatGPT, GPT-4, Claude, or other AI tools with 95%+ accuracy

3.3 WHEN comparing student submissions, THE Collusion Detection AI SHALL identify unusual similarities in structure, logic, or phrasing that suggest unauthorized collaboration

3.4 WHEN monitoring exam sessions, THE Proctoring AI SHALL analyze webcam footage, eye movements, and behavior patterns to detect cheating attempts in real-time

3.5 WHEN flagging potential violations, THE System SHALL provide evidence packages for human review including similarity scores, highlighted sections, and behavioral anomalies

### Requirement 4: Personalized Learning Path Optimization

**User Story:** As a student, I want AI to adapt my learning path based on my progress so that I'm always challenged at the right level and can learn efficiently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

4.1 WHEN a student completes assessments, THE Learning Analytics AI SHALL analyze performance patterns to identify strengths, weaknesses, and learning style preferences

4.2 WHEN generating study recommendations, THE Adaptive Learning AI SHALL suggest specific resources, practice problems, and learning activities tailored to individual student needs

4.3 WHEN a student struggles with a concept, THE Intervention AI SHALL automatically trigger additional support including tutoring sessions, supplementary materials, and peer study groups

4.4 WHEN optimizing course sequences, THE Path Optimization AI SHALL recommend course ordering and pacing based on student's background, goals, and learning velocity

4.5 WHEN predicting outcomes, THE Predictive Analytics AI SHALL identify students at risk of failing or dropping out with 80%+ accuracy, enabling early intervention

### Requirement 5: Automated Student Support & FAQ

**User Story:** As a student, I want instant answers to common questions so that I don't have to wait for support staff during nights and weekends.

#### Acceptance Criteria

5.1 WHEN a student asks a question via chat, email, or support portal, THE Support AI SHALL provide accurate answers by searching knowledge base, course materials, and university policies

5.2 WHEN answering questions, THE Support AI SHALL maintain conversational context across multiple messages and remember previous interactions with the student

5.3 WHEN unable to answer confidently, THE Support AI SHALL escalate to human support staff with full conversation context and suggested priority level

5.4 WHEN detecting urgent issues, THE Support AI SHALL immediately alert appropriate staff via SMS and create high-priority tickets

5.5 WHEN interacting with students, THE Support AI SHALL maintain warm, encouraging tone consistent with ScrollUniversity's Christian mission and values

### Requirement 6: Intelligent Admissions Processing

**User Story:** As an admissions officer, I want AI to screen and evaluate applications so that I can focus on borderline cases and personal interviews.

#### Acceptance Criteria

6.1 WHEN an application is submitted, THE Application Screening AI SHALL extract and structure data from essays, transcripts, resumes, and recommendation letters

6.2 WHEN evaluating applications, THE Admissions AI SHALL score candidates on academic qualifications, spiritual maturity, leadership potential, and mission alignment using rubric

6.3 WHEN analyzing essays, THE Essay Evaluation AI SHALL assess writing quality, authenticity, spiritual depth, and alignment with ScrollUniversity values

6.4 WHEN applications score above 85%, THE System SHALL automatically recommend acceptance with scholarship consideration

6.5 WHEN applications score below 40%, THE System SHALL generate personalized rejection letters with constructive feedback and alternative pathways

### Requirement 7: Automated Research Assistance

**User Story:** As a student researcher, I want AI to help with literature review and research methodology so that I can produce publication-quality work efficiently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

7.1 WHEN a student provides a research topic, THE Research Assistant AI SHALL identify relevant papers, summarize key findings, and map the research landscape

7.2 WHEN analyzing papers, THE Literature Review AI SHALL extract methodologies, findings, limitations, and connections between studies

7.3 WHEN designing studies, THE Methodology AI SHALL suggest appropriate research methods, statistical analyses, and potential confounding variables

7.4 WHEN writing papers, THE Citation AI SHALL automatically format citations in APA, MLA, or Chicago style and verify citation accuracy

7.5 WHEN reviewing drafts, THE Research Feedback AI SHALL provide feedback on argument structure, evidence quality, and academic writing standards

8.3 WHEN students change majors, THE Transfer Credit AI SHALL automatically map completed courses to new requirements and identify gaps

8.4 WHEN planning schedules, THE Schedule Optimization AI SHALL balance course load, prevent burnout, and maximize learning effectiveness

8.5 WHEN advising on electives, THE Career Alignment AI SHALL recommend courses that align with student's career aspirations and industry demands
