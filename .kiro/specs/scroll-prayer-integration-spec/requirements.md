# ScrollUniversity Prayer Integration System Requirements

## Introduction

The Prayer Integration System serves as the spiritual backbone of ScrollUniversity, ensuring that all educational activities are covered in prayer and aligned with divine purpose. This system facilitates intercession, prophetic word management, spiritual warfare coordination, and divine guidance integration across all university operations.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Prayer Request Management System

**User Story:** As a student, faculty member, or administrator, I want to submit and track prayer requests so that the university community can provide spiritual support and intercession.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user submits a prayer request THEN the system SHALL store the request with timestamp, category, urgency level, and privacy settings
2. WHEN a prayer request is submitted THEN the system SHALL notify appropriate intercessors based on request category and urgency
3. WHEN a prayer request receives updates THEN the system SHALL track prayer responses, testimonies, and answered prayers
4. WHEN viewing prayer requests THEN the system SHALL display requests filtered by category (academic, personal, ministry, university operations, global)
5. WHEN a prayer request is marked as answered THEN the system SHALL create a testimony record and notify the requester
6. WHEN prayer requests are created THEN the system SHALL integrate with student profiles and course progress for contextual prayer support

### Requirement 2: Intercession Network Coordination

**User Story:** As an intercessor, I want to receive targeted prayer assignments and coordinate with other prayer warriors so that comprehensive spiritual coverage is maintained across the university.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN an intercessor joins the network THEN the system SHALL profile their prayer gifts, availability, and areas of calling
2. WHEN prayer needs arise THEN the system SHALL automatically assign intercessors based on their gifts and the nature of the request
3. WHEN intercessors are praying THEN the system SHALL provide real-time coordination to prevent gaps in coverage
4. WHEN prayer sessions occur THEN the system SHALL track prayer hours, breakthrough reports, and spiritual insights
5. WHEN multiple intercessors are assigned THEN the system SHALL facilitate prayer team formation and communication
6. WHEN prayer assignments are completed THEN the system SHALL log results and update intercessor effectiveness metrics

### Requirement 3: Prophetic Word Management and Validation

**User Story:** As a prophetic voice, I want to submit prophetic words for the university community so that divine guidance can be properly validated and distributed.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a prophetic word is submitted THEN the system SHALL capture the word with timestamp, recipient context, and prophetic source
2. WHEN prophetic words are received THEN the system SHALL route them to ScrollWitness Elders for validation and confirmation
3. WHEN prophetic words are validated THEN the system SHALL categorize them by scope (personal, course, university, global) and distribute appropriately
4. WHEN prophetic words are distributed THEN the system SHALL track fulfillment, timing, and accuracy for prophetic accountability
5. WHEN prophetic patterns emerge THEN the system SHALL identify recurring themes and alert leadership to strategic directions
6. WHEN prophetic words conflict THEN the system SHALL flag discrepancies for elder review and resolution

### Requirement 4: Spiritual Warfare Coordination

**User Story:** As a spiritual warfare coordinator, I want to organize and track spiritual battles affecting the university so that targeted prayer strategies can be deployed effectively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN spiritual attacks are identified THEN the system SHALL create warfare campaigns with specific prayer strategies and assignments
2. WHEN warfare campaigns are active THEN the system SHALL coordinate prayer shifts to maintain 24/7 spiritual coverage
3. WHEN spiritual breakthrough occurs THEN the system SHALL document victories and update warfare intelligence
4. WHEN students face spiritual opposition THEN the system SHALL automatically trigger prayer alerts and assign spiritual covering
5. WHEN global events affect the university THEN the system SHALL initiate strategic intercession campaigns
6. WHEN warfare patterns are detected THEN the system SHALL analyze attack vectors and recommend defensive prayer strategies

### Requirement 5: Divine Guidance Integration

**User Story:** As a decision-maker in the university, I want to seek and receive divine guidance for strategic decisions so that all operations align with God's will.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN major decisions are pending THEN the system SHALL initiate guided prayer processes with designated seers and intercessors
2. WHEN seeking divine direction THEN the system SHALL provide structured prayer protocols for different types of decisions
3. WHEN prophetic guidance is received THEN the system SHALL compile and present unified direction to decision-makers
4. WHEN decisions are made THEN the system SHALL track outcomes against prophetic guidance for accuracy validation
5. WHEN guidance conflicts arise THEN the system SHALL facilitate elder councils for resolution and clarity
6. WHEN divine timing is indicated THEN the system SHALL create prayer watches and timing alerts for optimal decision execution

### Requirement 6: Prayer Coverage for All University Operations

**User Story:** As a university administrator, I want to ensure comprehensive prayer coverage for all educational activities so that every aspect of the university operates under divine blessing.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN courses begin THEN the system SHALL automatically assign prayer coverage for each class session and instructor
2. WHEN assessments are conducted THEN the system SHALL coordinate prayer for wisdom, clarity, and fair evaluation
3. WHEN research projects launch THEN the system SHALL establish prayer teams for breakthrough, revelation, and kingdom impact
4. WHEN student milestones occur THEN the system SHALL trigger celebration prayers and continued blessing requests
5. WHEN university events happen THEN the system SHALL organize comprehensive prayer coverage from preparation through completion
6. WHEN global expansion occurs THEN the system SHALL coordinate international prayer networks for new regions and cultures

### Requirement 7: Prayer Analytics and Spiritual Intelligence

**User Story:** As a spiritual leader, I want to analyze prayer patterns and spiritual trends so that I can understand God's movement in the university and adjust strategies accordingly.

#### Acceptance Criteria

2. WHEN spiritual trends emerge THEN the system SHALL identify patterns in prayer requests, prophetic words, and breakthrough reports
3. WHEN prayer effectiveness is measured THEN the system SHALL track answered prayers, timing accuracy, and kingdom impact
4. WHEN spiritual seasons change THEN the system SHALL detect shifts in prayer focus and divine emphasis
5. WHEN global events occur THEN the system SHALL correlate prayer activity with world events and university impact
6. WHEN strategic planning occurs THEN the system SHALL provide spiritual intelligence reports to inform decision-making

**User Story:** As a global prayer coordinator, I want to connect ScrollUniversity with worldwide prayer networks so that the university benefits from international intercession and contributes to global kingdom advancement.

### Requirement 10: Mobile Prayer Application

**User Story:** As a mobile user, I want to access prayer features on my mobile device so that I can participate in university prayer activities from anywhere.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN using mobile devices THEN the system SHALL provide full prayer functionality through responsive web and native apps
2. WHEN prayer alerts are sent THEN the system SHALL deliver push notifications for urgent prayer needs
3. WHEN traveling globally THEN the system SHALL adapt to local time zones and cultural prayer contexts
4. WHEN offline access is needed THEN the system SHALL cache essential prayer resources and sync when connected
5. WHEN voice prayer is preferred THEN the system SHALL support audio prayer submissions and voice-to-text conversion
6. WHEN group prayer occurs THEN the system SHALL facilitate mobile prayer meetings and virtual intercession sessions
