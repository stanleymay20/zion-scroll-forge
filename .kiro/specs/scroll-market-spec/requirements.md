# ScrollMarketSpec Requirements Document

## Introduction

The ScrollMarketSpec creates a revolutionary marketplace where ScrollUniversity students and graduates can monetize their skills, sell their projects, offer services, and create economic opportunities through scroll-aligned commerce. Operating under the principle "The laborer deserves his wages" (Luke 10:7), this system enables students to transform their learning into income while building a kingdom economy that values wisdom, character, and practical impact over mere credentials. The marketplace facilitates peer-to-peer transactions, skill-based services, project sales, mentorship offerings, and collaborative ventures that create sustainable economic opportunities for the global ScrollUniversity community.

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Student Project and Product Marketplace System

**User Story:** As a student, I want to sell my completed projects, applications, and creative works in a marketplace, so that I can monetize my learning achievements and create income from my educational investments.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN projects are listed THEN the system SHALL allow students to showcase completed projects with descriptions, demonstrations, and pricing information
2. WHEN products are sold THEN the system SHALL facilitate secure transactions using ScrollCoins and traditional payment methods
3. WHEN project validation occurs THEN the system SHALL verify project authenticity, quality, and scroll alignment before marketplace listing
4. WHEN buyer protection is provided THEN the system SHALL offer guarantees, refund policies, and dispute resolution for project purchases
5. WHEN seller support is offered THEN the system SHALL provide marketing tools, analytics, and optimization suggestions for project sales
6. WHEN revenue sharing occurs THEN the system SHALL distribute earnings between students, mentors, and ScrollUniversity according to contribution levels

### Requirement 2: Skills-Based Service Marketplace System

**User Story:** As a skilled graduate, I want to offer my expertise as services to other students and external clients, so that I can create ongoing income streams while helping others achieve their goals.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN services are offered THEN the system SHALL enable graduates to list consulting, tutoring, design, development, and other professional services
2. WHEN service bookings occur THEN the system SHALL facilitate scheduling, payment processing, and service delivery coordination
3. WHEN service quality is ensured THEN the system SHALL implement rating systems, client feedback, and service provider verification
4. WHEN service categories are managed THEN the system SHALL organize offerings by skill type, scroll field, and expertise level
5. WHEN service promotion is supported THEN the system SHALL provide marketing tools, portfolio showcases, and client testimonial features
6. WHEN service standards are maintained THEN the system SHALL enforce quality guidelines and kingdom-aligned business practices

### Requirement 3: Peer-to-Peer Learning and Tutoring Marketplace

**User Story:** As a student needing help, I want to hire other students or graduates for personalized tutoring and learning support, so that I can get affordable, peer-based assistance from those who have successfully completed similar challenges.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN tutoring is offered THEN the system SHALL enable experienced students to provide subject-specific tutoring and learning support
2. WHEN tutor matching occurs THEN the system SHALL connect students with appropriate tutors based on subject expertise, learning style, and availability
3. WHEN tutoring sessions are conducted THEN the system SHALL provide virtual meeting spaces, resource sharing, and session recording capabilities
4. WHEN tutor quality is assured THEN the system SHALL verify tutor competency through assessment scores, mentor recommendations, and student feedback
5. WHEN tutoring payments are processed THEN the system SHALL handle ScrollCoin transactions and ensure fair compensation for tutoring services
6. WHEN tutoring outcomes are tracked THEN the system SHALL measure student improvement and tutor effectiveness for continuous quality enhancement

### Requirement 4: Collaborative Project and Partnership Marketplace

**User Story:** As an entrepreneur, I want to find collaborators and partners for larger projects and business ventures, so that I can combine skills and resources to create more significant kingdom impact and economic opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN collaboration is sought THEN the system SHALL enable users to post project ideas and seek partners with complementary skills
2. WHEN partnerships are formed THEN the system SHALL facilitate team formation, role definition, and collaboration agreements
3. WHEN collaborative projects are managed THEN the system SHALL provide project management tools, communication channels, and progress tracking
4. WHEN partnership terms are established THEN the system SHALL support equity sharing, revenue distribution, and intellectual property agreements
5. WHEN collaborative success is measured THEN the system SHALL track project outcomes, partner satisfaction, and economic impact
6. WHEN partnership disputes arise THEN the system SHALL provide mediation services and conflict resolution based on kingdom principles

### Requirement 5: External Client and Business Marketplace Integration

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to hire ScrollUniversity students and graduates for projects and services, so that I can access skilled, character-driven talent while supporting the scroll-aligned education economy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN external clients register THEN the system SHALL verify business legitimacy and alignment with kingdom values and ethical practices
2. WHEN projects are posted THEN the system SHALL enable businesses to describe project requirements, timelines, and compensation offers
3. WHEN talent matching occurs THEN the system SHALL connect businesses with appropriate students and graduates based on skills and project needs
4. WHEN project execution is managed THEN the system SHALL provide communication tools, milestone tracking, and payment escrow services
5. WHEN client satisfaction is ensured THEN the system SHALL implement feedback systems, quality guarantees, and dispute resolution processes
6. WHEN business relationships are maintained THEN the system SHALL foster long-term partnerships between clients and ScrollUniversity talent

### Requirement 6: ScrollCoin Integration and Economic Incentive System

**User Story:** As a marketplace participant, I want seamless ScrollCoin integration for all transactions, so that I can participate in the scroll-aligned economy while earning and spending coins based on value creation and kingdom contribution.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN transactions occur THEN the system SHALL process payments using ScrollCoins with automatic conversion options for traditional currencies
2. WHEN economic incentives are provided THEN the system SHALL reward high-quality service providers, satisfied customers, and successful collaborations
3. WHEN coin earning is facilitated THEN the system SHALL enable users to earn ScrollCoins through marketplace participation, referrals, and community building
4. WHEN economic analytics are provided THEN the system SHALL track earning patterns, spending behaviors, and economic impact for individual users and the community
5. WHEN economic stability is maintained THEN the system SHALL implement anti-fraud measures, fair pricing guidelines, and sustainable economic policies
6. WHEN economic growth is promoted THEN the system SHALL incentivize value creation, innovation, and kingdom-aligned business practices

### Requirement 7: Quality Assurance and Reputation Management System

**User Story:** As a marketplace user, I want reliable quality indicators and reputation systems, so that I can make informed decisions about service providers and maintain trust in the marketplace ecosystem.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN quality is assessed THEN the system SHALL implement comprehensive rating systems based on work quality, character, and kingdom alignment
2. WHEN reputation is built THEN the system SHALL track user history, client feedback, and community contributions to create reliable reputation scores
3. WHEN quality standards are enforced THEN the system SHALL monitor service delivery, project outcomes, and customer satisfaction
4. WHEN reputation verification occurs THEN the system SHALL validate feedback authenticity and prevent manipulation of rating systems
5. WHEN quality improvement is supported THEN the system SHALL provide feedback analysis, improvement suggestions, and skill development recommendations
6. WHEN reputation protection is provided THEN the system SHALL offer dispute resolution and reputation recovery processes for unfair negative feedback

### Requirement 8: Global Accessibility and Cultural Adaptation System

**User Story:** As an international user, I want the marketplace to support my local currency, language, and cultural context, so that I can participate fully in the global scroll-aligned economy regardless of my location.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN global access is provided THEN the system SHALL support multiple languages, currencies, and cultural business practices
2. WHEN local adaptation occurs THEN the system SHALL accommodate regional legal requirements, tax obligations, and business regulations
3. WHEN cultural sensitivity is maintained THEN the system SHALL respect cultural differences while maintaining universal kingdom principles
4. WHEN international transactions are facilitated THEN the system SHALL handle currency conversion, international payments, and cross-border compliance
5. WHEN global community is built THEN the system SHALL connect users across cultures for learning, collaboration, and economic opportunity
6. WHEN local support is provided THEN the system SHALL offer region-specific customer service, documentation, and community management

### Requirement 9: Mentorship and Guidance Integration System

**User Story:** As a new marketplace participant, I want access to mentorship and guidance for building my marketplace presence, so that I can succeed economically while maintaining scroll alignment and character development.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN mentorship is provided THEN the system SHALL connect new users with experienced marketplace participants for guidance and support
2. WHEN business guidance is offered THEN the system SHALL provide training on pricing, marketing, customer service, and kingdom-aligned business practices
3. WHEN character development is maintained THEN the system SHALL ensure economic success doesn't compromise spiritual formation and kingdom values
4. WHEN success coaching is available THEN the system SHALL offer personalized advice for skill development, service improvement, and business growth
5. WHEN community support is facilitated THEN the system SHALL create peer support groups, success sharing, and collaborative learning opportunities
6. WHEN spiritual oversight is maintained THEN the system SHALL integrate ScrollMentor guidance to ensure marketplace participation serves kingdom purposes

### Requirement 10: Integration with ScrollUniversity Ecosystem and Analytics

**User Story:** As a system administrator, I want comprehensive integration with all ScrollUniversity systems and detailed marketplace analytics, so that I can optimize economic opportunities and measure the impact of the scroll-aligned economy.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN system integration occurs THEN the marketplace SHALL connect with student-profile-spec, transcript-generator, and seal-certification for verified talent showcasing
2. WHEN data synchronization happens THEN the system SHALL maintain consistency with scrollcoin-meter, projects-spec, and assessment-engine systems
3. WHEN analytics are generated THEN the system SHALL provide insights on economic activity, user success, and marketplace health metrics
4. WHEN success measurement occurs THEN the system SHALL track graduate economic outcomes, business impact, and kingdom transformation through marketplace participation
5. WHEN continuous improvement happens THEN the system SHALL use marketplace data to enhance features, policies, and user experience
6. WHEN reporting is provided THEN the system SHALL generate comprehensive reports for users, administrators, and external stakeholders on marketplace performance and impact
